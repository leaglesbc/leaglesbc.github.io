Contrast Repetition Alignment Proximity
JUMBOTRON

Contrast allows you to emphasize or highlight key elements within your design. Contrast is created when two elements are total opposites.

The principle of repetition simply means the reusing of the same or similar elements throughout your design.

Alignment is one of the most basic and important principles of design. It allows us to create order and organisation among elements.

Proximity helps create organisation. By grouping similar elements together or in close proximity, you create a relationship between those elements.
